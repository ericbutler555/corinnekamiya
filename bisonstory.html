i tell this story over and over.
                                          i didn't know that bison were real, i mean-- i knew they were real at some point, i just thought that
                                          they were extinct... the saying 'slowly going the way of the buffalo' makes them seem like they are
                                          dead and gone. so i had never really given this a second thought. i had never happened to run into
                                          any in hawaii, or anywhere else i had visited on the mainland, so i assumed-- they must be all dead.
                                        

                                                   and then. i was twenty-three, and living in california, and visiting golden gate park, and if you didn't
                                                        know. there is a bison field in the park. it was amazing. my internal dialog went something like--
                                                                               "oh my god-- they're REAL!"
