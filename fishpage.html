i gave a fish to everyone in my graduate class. one person cried.
others were happy to have a pet, someone waited until the end
to pick the 'ugliest' fish that would hopefully live the longest. (which
by some strange coincidence has been true.) and it has been a 
year now. many of the fish have died, others have changed color,
some lived with my turtles for the winter break. (although i had to
evacuate the fish when the turtles started getting hungry in spring
and ate one of the fish.) overall it has been a strange project about
giving a gift that asks a lot back.
